Angelic Pretty

Angelic Pretty is a Japanese brand of Lolita fashion.
Lolita is a fashion subculture from Japan that is highly influenced by Victorian clothing and styles from the Rococo period.
Here are some pictures of dresses from Angelic Pretty. You can get an idea how Lolita fashion looks like if you never heard of it.

Besides these sweet dresses, there are also other types of Lolita fashion.

  • Gothic
  • Classic
  • Sailor
  • Hime(princess)
  • ...A lot more
